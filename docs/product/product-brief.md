# Product Brief

## Name

Orvex

## One-Liner

Orvex is a multimodal inspection copilot for solar operations teams that analyzes thermal and RGB panel images, identifies suspected anomalies, prioritizes maintenance risk, and generates structured reports for human review.

## Problem

Solar operations teams receive large volumes of visual, thermal, and drone inspection imagery. Manual triage is slow, inconsistent, and difficult to convert into prioritized maintenance work.

The core user problem is not just detecting an isolated defect. The product must help answer:

- which assets need attention first;
- what visual evidence supports the finding;
- what defect is suspected;
- what action should be reviewed by maintenance;
- where human review is required.

## Initial Users

- Solar operations and maintenance teams.
- Field technicians reviewing inspection media.
- Drone inspection providers.
- Asset managers responsible for solar portfolios.

## Future Buyers

- Photovoltaic plant operators.
- Solar O&M providers.
- Drone inspection companies.
- Energy infrastructure software integrators.

## Value Proposition

Orvex reduces inspection triage time by turning raw images into a prioritized queue of human-reviewable findings and preliminary maintenance reports.

Practical value:

- less time reviewing images with no visible issue;
- consistent severity and priority workflow;
- structured evidence for audit and follow-up;
- human-in-the-loop review before action;
- foundation for historical comparison by asset.

## Safe Positioning

Use:

- AI-assisted visual triage;
- maintenance prioritization;
- preliminary inspection report;
- human-in-the-loop workflow;
- multimodal evidence analysis.

Avoid:

- definitive diagnosis;
- technician replacement;
- guaranteed fault detection;
- legal, warranty, or insurance conclusions;
- unvalidated accuracy claims.

## Commercial Hypothesis

If validated, Orvex can evolve into a vertical SaaS for photovoltaic inspection and maintenance workflows.

Potential pricing models:

- per solar site;
- per MW monitored;
- per inspection batch;
- per image volume;
- enterprise plan for drone/O&M providers with integrations.
